# Cabinet impulse responses

This folder is intentionally empty. Drop `.wav` cab IRs in here and they'll
appear in the NAM module's "Choose Cabinet" picker.

NAM amp-only models don't include speaker cabinet modeling, so pairing an amp
model with an IR is what makes it sound finished.

## Free packs worth grabbing

Couldn't find any guitar-cab IR pack with a license clean enough to bundle
here, but these are good starting points:

- **[TONE3000 — free IRs](https://tone3000.com/search?categories=ir)** —
  thousands of community uploads, browseable by gear and creator.
- **[Redwirez Marshall 1960A free pack](https://redwirez.com/pages/the-marshall-1960a-ir-pack)** —
  Celestion G12M-25s, 17 mic positions. Free with email signup.
- **[OwnHammer free cab IRs](https://www.ownhammer.com/free/cab-irs/)** —
  free starter pack from a respected commercial IR maker.
- **[Conner's IR Library (MIT)](https://github.com/itsmusician/IR-Library)** —
  738 IRs under MIT, including the Roland JC-120 cab, spring/plate reverbs,
  and a lot of creative material. Light on classic guitar cabs but fully
  redistributable.

The first three are "free download, personal use" — fine to use in your own
patches, but check the EULA before redistributing or selling tones built on
them. Conner's library is the only one of the four you can repackage.

## Beyond cabinets

The "cab" slot is just a convolver, so anything `.wav` works here — try a
spring or plate reverb IR to add room or shimmer in front of your amp
model. Most reverbs belong in a dedicated FX module (see Space Echo,
CloudSeed, PSXVerb), but a quick IR convolution can be a nice touch.

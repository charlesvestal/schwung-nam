# Cabinet impulse responses

This folder is intentionally empty. Drop `.wav` cab IRs in here and they'll
appear in the NAM module's "Choose Cabinet" picker.

NAM amp-only models don't include speaker cabinet modeling, so pairing an amp
model with an IR is what makes it sound finished.

## Where to find free IRs

- <https://tone3000.com> — community captures, NAM models and IRs side by side.
- Most amp builders publish free IR packs of their own cabinets — search
  "free guitar cab IR" for current options.

Check the per-pack license before redistributing. "Free download" usually means
personal use, not redistribution.
